# ❓ Madrid Itinerary Questions for the Group

To finalize the exact schedule for our newly added **Madrid** leg (Sept 23 - Sept 27), please discuss the following questions as a group and let me know the consensus!

## 🏨 1. Accommodation Vibes
Where do we want our home base in Madrid to be? 
* **Malasaña / Chueca**: Super trendy, hipster, incredible nightlife, vintage shops, loud and vibrant.
* **La Latina**: Very traditional, older architecture, famous for tapas and Sunday markets.
* **Centro (Sol / Plaza Mayor)**: Right in the middle of all the tourist attractions. Very convenient but very busy and touristy.
* **Retiro / Salamanca**: Upscale, quiet, beautiful streets, right next to the massive Retiro park. 

## 🚆 2. Day Trip to Toledo?
On Day 14 (Friday), we have a full free day. Do we want to:
* **Option A**: Stay in Madrid. Go to the world-famous Prado Museum and relax in El Retiro Park.
* **Option B**: Take a 33-minute high-speed train to **Toledo**, an incredible ancient medieval city on a hill. It’s gorgeous but involves some transit and walking.
