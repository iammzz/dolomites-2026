# 🗓️ The Ultimate Dolomites 2026 Itinerary


This overview merges the absolute best of your current plans, the Google Sheet, and the Earth Trekkers recommendations into one seamless, perfectly-paced 14-day trip. We have shifted the schedule to give you 7 full days in the Western Dolomites.

---

## 🏔️ Part 1: Eastern Dolomites (Base: Cortina d'Ampezzo)

**Day 1 | Sept 12: Arrival in Venice**
* **Morning**: Arrive at VCE (11:30 AM). 
* **Afternoon**: City touring, canals, dinner in Venice. 
* **Rationale**: One night in Venice is sensible. Pick up your car at the airport to avoid a second trip back to the airport.
* *Sleep: Venice*

**Day 2 | Sept 13: The Scenic Drive North**
* **Morning**: Pick up rental car at Piazzale Roma. Drive to Conegliano.
* **Afternoon**: Drive the **Prosecco Hills (Strada del Prosecco)** via Valdobbiadene. 
* **Late Afternoon**: Arrive in Cortina, check-in, grab groceries.
* **Rationale**: The Prosecco Hills (Conegliano-Valdobbiadene) are beautiful and right on the route. Budget 1.5–2 hours there, and you'll still make Cortina by 3–4pm.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

**Day 3 | Sept 14: WWI History & Towers**
* **Morning**: Drive to Baita Bai de Dones. Chairlift up to **Cinque Torri**. 
* **Midday**: Hike the loop and up to Rifugio Nuvolau for lunch with 360° views.
* **Afternoon**: Drive to **Passo Giau** for sunset photography.
* **Rationale**: This is ET's top recommendation for a full, satisfying day. Cinque Torri and Rifugio Averau/Nuvolau form a perfect morning. Passo Giau is one of the most dramatic drives in the Dolomites and is great for sunset.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

**Day 4 | Sept 15: The Iconic Peaks**
* **Morning (EARLY)**: Drive the toll road to Rifugio Auronzo (arrive by 7:30 AM).
* **Morning**: Hike the **Tre Cime di Lavaredo Loop** (10 km).
* **Afternoon**: Add on the **Cadini di Misurina Viewpoint** (3 km) from the same parking lot.
* **Rationale**: This is the absolute signature hike of the Dolomites. You must arrive at the toll booth very early (before 8 AM) to secure parking, otherwise you will be turned away.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

**Day 5 | Sept 16: The Glacial Lake**
* **Morning**: Drive to Passo Tre Croci.
* **Midday**: Hike to **Lago di Sorapis** (12 km return). 
* **Afternoon**: Relax in Cortina town center.
* **Rationale**: Famous for its milky-blue water. The hike has steep inclines and metal stairs, so it is physically demanding.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

---

## 🚗 Part 2: The Epic Transit Day

**Day 6 | Sept 17: Cortina to Val Gardena via The Passes**
* **Morning**: Drive to Falzarego Pass. Cable car up to **Lagazuoi**. Explore the WWI tunnels.
* **Midday**: Drive south to **Marmolada**. Cable car to Punta Rocca (highest peak) and see the Museum of the Great War.
* **Afternoon**: Drive Passo Fedaia → Passo Pordoi → **Sass Pordoi Cable Car** → Sella Pass.
* **Evening**: Arrive in Val Gardena.
* **Rationale**: ET calls this one of the most scenic drive days of the trip. The Marmolada cable car and Museum of the Great War perfectly complement the Lagazuoi WWI tunnels.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena (Ortisei/Selva)*

---

## ⛰️ Part 3: Western Dolomites (Base: Val Gardena)

**Day 7 | Sept 18: Val di Funes & The Geisler Peaks**
* **Morning**: Paragliding (if booked) or explore St. Magdalena village.
* **Midday**: Hike the **Adolf Munkel Weg** (9.2 km) right beneath the Odle peaks. 
* **Afternoon**: Stop at the Santa Maddalena Church viewpoint for the classic photo.
* **Rationale**: Val di Funes is an incredible valley. Adding the quick 5-minute detour to the Church of St. John gives you the iconic postcard shot of the Dolomites.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena*

**Day 8 | Sept 19: The Highest Alpine Meadow**
* **Morning**: Take the cable car up to **Seiser Alm (Alpe di Siusi)**.
* **Midday**: Hike or rent e-bikes to hop between rifugios for food and views.
* **Afternoon**: Drive to the medieval village of **Castelrotto**.
* **Rationale**: The road up to Seiser Alm is closed to private cars from 9am to 5pm, so pre-booking the cable car is essential. Renting bikes makes the massive plateau much more fun.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena*

**Day 9 | Sept 20: The Postcard Ridge**
* **Morning**: Cable car up to **Seceda**. 
* **Midday**: Walk the ridge line toward Pieralongia. Take in the plunging cliffs.
* **Afternoon**: If you didn't finish the Sella Pass drive on transit day, drive it today. Otherwise, explore Ortisei.
* **Rationale**: Seceda is the postcard image of the western Dolomites. Don't just go up and down—walk the ridge trail for at least 1-2 hours.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena*

## ✈️ Part 4: Departure

**Day 10 | Sept 21: To Madrid**
* **Morning**: 4-hour drive back to Venice Marco Polo (VCE) airport.
* **Afternoon**: Flight to Madrid.
* **Evening**: Arrive in Madrid, rest. (Anyma concert is Sept 26).
