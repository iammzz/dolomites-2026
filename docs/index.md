# 🗓️ The Ultimate Dolomites 2026 Itinerary

This overview merges the absolute best of your plans into a seamless, perfectly-paced 12-day trip. We start in the Western Dolomites and slowly make our way East, breaking up the most intense days with "Split-Group" rest days!

## ⛰️ Part 1: Western Dolomites (Base: Val Gardena)

**Day 1 | Saturday, Sept 12: Arrival in Venice**
* **Morning**: Arrive at Venice Marco Polo (VCE) via TS 306 at 11:30 AM. 
* **Midday**: Pick up your rental car at the airport. Ensure it fits all luggage!
* **Afternoon**: Check into your Venice/Mestre hotel. Rest and recover from jetlag.
* *Sleep: Venice / Mestre*

**Day 2 | Sunday, Sept 13: Drive North & Settle In**
* **Morning**: Drive from Venice to Val Gardena (approx. 3 hours via A22).
* **Midday**: Arrive in Ortisei, check into your base, and grab groceries at Despar.
* **Afternoon**: Walk around town, or ride the Resciesa funicular for an easy warmup walk to get adjusted to the altitude.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena (Ortisei)*

**Day 3 | Monday, Sept 14: Val di Funes & The Geisler Peaks**
* **Morning**: Paragliding (if booked) or explore St. Magdalena village.
* **Midday**: Hike the **Adolf Munkel Weg** (9.2 km) right beneath the Odle peaks. 
* **Afternoon**: Stop at the Santa Maddalena Church viewpoint for the classic photo.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena (Ortisei)*

**Day 4 | Tuesday, Sept 15: The Highest Alpine Meadow**
* **Morning**: Take the cable car up to **Seiser Alm (Alpe di Siusi)**.
* **Midday**: Rent e-bikes to hop between rifugios for food and views.
* **Afternoon**: Drive to the medieval village of **Castelrotto** on the way back down.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena (Ortisei)*

**Day 5 | Wednesday, Sept 16: The Postcard Ridge**
* **Morning**: Cable car up to **Seceda**. 
* **Midday**: Walk the ridge line toward Pieralongia. Take in the plunging cliffs.
* **Afternoon**: Relax back at the base.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena (Ortisei)*

**Day 6 | Thursday, Sept 17: West Split Day**
* **Thrill Group**: Via Ferrata Gran Cir (Passo Gardena).
* **Relax Group**: Ortisei town exploration, Bolzano (Ötzi Museum), or Spa day.
* *Sleep: Val Gardena (Ortisei)*

---

## 🚗 Part 2: The Epic Transit Day

**Day 7 | Friday, Sept 18: Val Gardena to Cortina via The Passes**
* **Morning**: Drive up the Sella Pass. Continue to **Marmolada**.
* **Midday**: Cable car to Punta Rocca (highest peak) and see the Museum of the Great War.
* **Afternoon**: Drive to Falzarego Pass. Cable car up to **Lagazuoi**. Explore the WWI tunnels.
* **Evening**: Arrive in Cortina.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

---

## 🏔️ Part 3: Eastern Dolomites (Base: Cortina d'Ampezzo)

**Day 8 | Saturday, Sept 19: WWI History & Towers**
* **Morning**: Drive to Baita Bai de Dones. Chairlift up to **Cinque Torri**. 
* **Midday**: Hike the loop and up to Rifugio Nuvolau for lunch with 360° views.
* **Afternoon**: Drive to **Passo Giau** for sunset photography.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

**Day 9 | Sunday, Sept 20: The Iconic Peaks**
* **Morning (EARLY)**: Drive the toll road to Rifugio Auronzo (arrive by 7:30 AM).
* **Morning**: Hike the **Tre Cime di Lavaredo Loop** (10 km).
* **Afternoon**: Add on the **Cadini di Misurina Viewpoint** (3 km) from the same parking lot.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

**Day 10 | Monday, Sept 21: East Split Day (Active Rest)**
* **Thrill Group**: Via Ferrata degli Alpini (Col dei Bos).
* **Relax Group**: Shopping in Cortina's Corso Italia or lunch at Lago di Misurina.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

**Day 11 | Tuesday, Sept 22: The Glacial Lake**
* **Morning**: Drive to Passo Tre Croci.
* **Midday**: Hike to **Lago di Sorapis** (12 km return). 
* **Afternoon**: Pack and relax.
* *Sleep: Cortina d'Ampezzo*

---

## ✈️ Part 4: Departure

**Day 12 | Wednesday, Sept 23: To Madrid**
* **Morning**: 2-hour drive back to Venice Marco Polo (VCE) airport.
* **Afternoon**: Flight to Madrid.
* **Evening**: Arrive in Madrid, rest. (Anyma concert is Sept 26).
