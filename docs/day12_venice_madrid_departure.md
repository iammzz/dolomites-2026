# Day 12 — Wednesday, Sept 23 ✈️
## Departure to Madrid
**Base:** Transit (Cortina d'Ampezzo → Venice VCE)

---

## 🏔️ Overview
Sadly, all good things must come to an end! Today you pack up your cars, leave the stunning peaks of the Dolomites behind, and head down to the Venetian lagoon to catch your flight to Madrid.

## 🚗 Morning: The Drive Down
* **Check-Out**: Pack the car and check out of your Cortina base.
* **The Route**: You will drive South from Cortina on the A27 highway. It is a very easy, straightforward drive out of the mountains, taking approximately 2 hours.
* **Refueling**: Make sure to stop at a gas station near the airport to fill up the rental car before dropping it off.

## ✈️ Midday: Airport Logistics
* **Rental Return**: Follow the signs for "Car Rental Return" at Venice Marco Polo Airport (VCE). Ensure you leave enough time for the inspection.
* **Check-In**: Check in for your flight to Madrid (MAD).

## 🇪🇸 Afternoon/Evening: Hello Spain!
* **Arrival**: Land in Madrid and check into your next accommodation.
* **Next Up**: Rest up and get ready for Anyma!
