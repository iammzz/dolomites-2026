# Day 12 — Wednesday, Sept 23 ✈️
## Departure to Madrid
**Base:** Transit (Cortina d'Ampezzo → Venice VCE)

---

## 🏔️ Overview
Sadly, all good things must come to an end! Today you pack up your cars, leave the stunning peaks of the Dolomites behind, and head down to the Venetian lagoon to catch your flight to Madrid.

## 🚗 Morning: The Drive Down & Car Return
* **8:30 AM**: Pack the car and check out of your Cortina base. You need to leave early to make it to Mestre by noon.
* **The Route**: You will drive South from Cortina on the A27 highway. It is a very easy, straightforward drive out of the mountains, taking approximately 2 to 2.5 hours.
* **Refueling**: Make sure to stop at a gas station near Mestre to fill up the rental car before dropping it off.
* **12:00 PM**: **Rental Return**: Drop off the car exactly at 12:00 PM at **Venice Mestre Rail Station** (as per your National Car Rental booking). 
* **Transit to Airport**: Take a quick train or taxi from Mestre Rail Station to Venice Marco Polo Airport (VCE) for your flight.

## ✈️ Midday: Airport Logistics
* **Check-In**: Check in for your flight to Madrid (MAD).

## 🇪🇸 Afternoon/Evening: Hello Spain!
* **Arrival**: Land in Madrid and check into your next accommodation.
* **Next Up**: Rest up and get ready for Anyma!
