## ✅ Chosen Afternoon Activity: Arrival in Cortina & Cable Car

### 1. Freccia nel Cielo Cable Car (Cima Tofana)
* **Distance**: N/A (Cable Car)
* **Elevation Change**: +1,344m (mechanical)
* **Duration**: 1.5–2 hrs
* **Difficulty**: ⭐ Easy (zero effort)
* **Extensions**: Once at the top (3,244m), you can walk out to the viewing platforms. There are also advanced Via Ferratas, but not recommended for arrival day.
* **Resources**:
  * [Earth Trekkers Guide](https://www.earthtrekkers.com/best-hikes-in-the-dolomites/)
  * [Official Website](https://www.freccianelcielo.com/en/)

**Expectation & Rationale:** 
Today is about the journey from Venice into the heart of the mountains. Driving through the Prosecco Hills makes the transit a joy. Once you arrive in Cortina, you will drop your bags and stretch your legs. If the skies are perfectly clear, taking the Freccia nel Cielo cable car right from town to the highest peak is the best zero-effort introduction to the Dolomites you can get. If it's cloudy, simply skip it and enjoy walking the town center.

> ⚠️ **Condition:** Only go if skies are fully clear. Check forecast morning of Sept 13.
