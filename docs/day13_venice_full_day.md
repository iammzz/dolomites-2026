# Day 13 — Thursday, Sept 24 🎭
## Venice Highlights
**Base:** Venice Island

---

## 🏛️ Overview
A full day dedicated to the Queen of the Adriatic. With no driving or hiking on the agenda, you can take your time soaking in the history, art, and romance of Venice.

## 🕊️ Morning: St. Mark's Square
* **Early Start**: Get out early before the massive cruise ship crowds arrive.
* **Piazza San Marco**: Visit the iconic St. Mark's Square. 
* **The Doge's Palace**: Highly recommended to book tickets in advance. Walk across the Bridge of Sighs and see the opulent rooms of the former rulers of Venice.
* **St. Mark's Basilica**: Admire the incredible golden mosaics. 

## 🍕 Midday: Rialto and Lunch
* **Rialto Bridge**: Walk toward the Rialto Bridge, stopping in small artisan shops along the way.
* **Lunch**: Cross the bridge and head into the San Polo district for lunch. Avoid restaurants directly on the main thoroughfares to get better quality and prices.

## 🛶 Afternoon: Canals or Islands
* **Option 1: Gondola Ride**: If you haven't done it yet, this is the time. It's touristy, but an essential Venice experience (around €80-€100 for 30 mins).
* **Option 2: Murano/Burano**: Take a Vaporetto out to the islands of Murano (famous for glass blowing) or Burano (famous for brightly colored houses and lace).

## 🍷 Evening: A Slower Pace
* Enjoy a nice sit-down seafood dinner (Venetian specialty) away from St. Mark's Square.
