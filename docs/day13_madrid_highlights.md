# Day 13 — Thursday, Sept 24 🇪🇸
## Madrid Historic Highlights
**Base:** Madrid

---

## 🏛️ Overview
Welcome to your first full day in Madrid! Today is dedicated to exploring the majestic historic center. The pace is yours to set—take time to wander, enjoy the beautiful Spanish architecture, and acclimate to the late-night Spanish lifestyle.

## 🚶‍♂️ Morning: The Heart of the City
* **Puerta del Sol**: Start your morning in the bustling center of Madrid. Grab a coffee and perhaps a *churro* to kickstart the day.
* **Plaza Mayor**: Walk just a few minutes over to the grand Plaza Mayor. Admire the frescoes on the Casa de la Panadería.
* **Mercado de San Miguel**: Just outside Plaza Mayor, walk through this famous indoor market. It's great for early tapas or simply admiring the beautifully displayed Iberian hams and cheeses.

## 👑 Midday: Royal Splendor
* **Royal Palace of Madrid (Palacio Real)**: Walk down Calle Mayor toward the Royal Palace. It's highly recommended to **pre-book tickets** to avoid the queue. The sheer scale and opulence of the rooms are incredible.
* **Almudena Cathedral**: Right next to the Palace, you can visit the cathedral (entrance is usually a small donation).
* **Lunch**: Find a nice menu del día (menu of the day) in the nearby Austrias neighborhood.

## 🍷 Evening: Tapas in La Latina
* **La Latina Neighborhood**: This is one of the oldest and most traditional neighborhoods in Madrid, famous for its tapas scene.
* **Calle de la Cava Baja**: Head to this street, which is lined wall-to-wall with tapas bars. The Spanish way is to have one drink and one tapa at a bar, then move on to the next one! Try some *patatas bravas*, *croquetas*, and *jamón ibérico*.
