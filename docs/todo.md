## Medium Priority (Book 2-3 Months Out)
- [ ] **International Driving Permit (IDP)**: [AAA IDP Portal](https://www.aaa.com/vacation/idpf.html)
  - *Nuance*: If your home driver's license is from outside the EU (e.g., Canada), you are legally required to carry an IDP to rent a car in Italy. Obtain this from CAA/AAA before you leave.
- [ ] **Reserve Tre Cime Parking**: [pass.auronzo.info](https://pass.auronzo.info)
  - *Nuance*: Parking at Rifugio Auronzo is strictly regulated and MUST be booked in advance online for a specific 12-hour slot (€40). You need your rental car license plate to book (though you can usually add it 24h before). Watch for when dates open!
- [ ] **Book Paragliding**: [Fly2 Dolomites](http://www.fly2.info)
  - *Nuance*: Highly weather dependent. Book early but be prepared to shift the day. Cost is around €110-€290 depending on flight length.
- [ ] **Book Spa Passes**: [QC Terme Dolomiti](https://www.qcterme.com/en/val-di-fassa/qc-terme-dolomiti) or local Ortisei Spas.
  - *Nuance*: Needed for the relax group on Split Days. Book a few weeks out.
- [ ] **Madrid Concert Tickets**: [Ticketmaster / Anyma Official]
  - *Nuance*: Ensure Anyma tickets are secured for the Madrid leg.
- [ ] **Book Key Restaurants**: 
  - *Lunch at Rifugio Averau*: [rifugioaverau.it](https://www.rifugioaverau.it/) - famous for its food on the Cinque Torri day. Email `rifugioaverau@gmail.com` to book.
  - *Dinner*: Any Michelin-star or highly rated restaurants in Cortina or Ortisei need a month's notice.

## Low Priority (1-2 Months Out)
- [ ] **Pre-Book Cable Car Tickets**: [Seceda Cable Car](https://www.seceda.it/en) / [Dolomiti SuperSummer](https://www.dolomitisuperski.com/en/SuperSummer)
  - *Nuance*: Cable cars don't sell out, but pre-booking online gives you a QR code to skip the morning ticket-buying lines. Alternatively, look into buying the *Dolomiti SuperSummer Card* or *Gardena Card* if the math makes sense for your total lift usage.
- [ ] **E-Bike Rentals**: [Rent and Go Ortisei](https://www.rentandgo.it/) or local shops.
  - *Nuance*: Reserve E-bikes in advance for the Alpe di Siusi day. Do not wait until you arrive, they will sell out in September.
- [ ] **Via Ferrata Gear**: [Sport Kostner (Ortisei)](https://www.sportkostner.com/) / [Snow Service (Cortina)](https://www.snowservice.it/)
  - *Nuance*: Map out rental shops for helmets, harnesses, and lanyards. Usually no need to book months out, but call 2-3 days ahead to reserve sizes.
- [ ] **Withdraw Cash (Euros)**: 
  - *Nuance*: Many mountain rifugios are **cash-only** due to poor cell service for card machines. Ensure you have plenty of Euros before heading up the mountains.
- [ ] **Review Trail Conditions**: [AllTrails](https://www.alltrails.com/)
  - *Nuance*: Check AllTrails and local tourism boards for trail closures or weather warnings right before you fly.
