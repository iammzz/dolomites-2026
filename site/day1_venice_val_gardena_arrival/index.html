    Day 1 — Saturday, Sept 12 🛬

    Arrival in Venice & Logistics Setup

    Base: Venice / Mestre

    ✅ Main Activity: Arrival & Recovery

    Expectation & Rationale: Today is about making it across the Atlantic, gathering your rental car, and recovering from jet lag before you tackle the mountains. You will be exhausted, so the goal is to keep things as low-stress as possible.

    1. The Airport Logistics (11:30 AM)

    • Arrival: Flight TS 306 lands at Venice Marco Polo Airport (VCE) at 11:30 AM.
    • Customs & Baggage (~1 hr): Budget around 1 hour to clear EU customs and grab your luggage.
    • Transit to Hotel (~20-30 min): Take a direct taxi from the airport to Venice-Mestre Beautiful Apartment (Via della Brenta Vecchia, 34). A taxi is approximately €35, which is the most convenient option for a group of 5 with heavy luggage. Alternatively, take the ATVO Airport Express bus (~€10) or ACTV Bus Line 15/45 (~€8) to central Mestre, followed by a short walk or taxi. Do NOT take the water bus, as your base is on the mainland.

    2. The Check-In (Afternoon)

    • Check-In: Settle into Venice-Mestre Beautiful Apartment (Via della Brenta Vecchia, 34). A special request has been put in for arrival between 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM (regular check-in is 14:00 - 21:00).
    • Rest: Check in, unpack, freshen up, and take a much-needed shower/nap.

    3. Evening in Venice (Optional — ~3 hrs)

    • If the group has energy, take a 15-minute train from Mestre Station to Venezia Santa Lucia (trains run every 5-10 min, ~€1.50). Walk around the canals near the station, grab some cicchetti (Venetian tapas) and Aperol Spritzes at a canal-side bacaro, and take the last train back. Head to bed early — tomorrow you drive into the mountains!

    ⚠️ Condition: Get to sleep early! Tomorrow you drive up into the high altitudes of the Dolomites.

    🍽️ Dining & Restaurant Options

    Recommendations based on your base in Mestre and an optional quick trip into Venice Island.

    Restaurant Food Type Typical Price Drive/Walk Distance Google Maps Link Why it's recommended
    Ristorante Casa Fortuna Authentic Venetian / Seafood €€ 5 min walk (0.3 km) 📍 Map Tucked away in a charming courtyard behind Piazza Ferretto, it's a local favorite for authentic Venetian seafood pasta and fresh lagoon catches.
    Ostaria da Mariano Venetian Seafood / Local €€ 5 min drive / 20 min walk (1.5 km) 📍 Map Family-run since 1963. Acclaimed for lagoon classics like peoci saltai (sautéed mussels), risotto di gò, and moeche (seasonal soft-shell crabs).
    Pizzium Neapolitan Pizza € 5 min walk (0.4 km) 📍 Map Located directly on Piazza Erminio Ferretto. Ideal for fresh, high-quality, modern wood-fired pizza when you want quick comfort food after your flight.
    Osteria Al Diplomatico Venetian Cicchetti / Bar €€ 10 min drive (3.5 km) 📍 Map A high-energy, buzzing tavern in Marghera with fantastic cicchetti (Venetian tapas like tuna meatballs, fried fish) and occasional live music.
    Bacareto da Lele Traditional Venetian Cicchetti € 25 min transit / 15 min drive (8.5 km) 📍 Map Located near Santa Lucia station on Venice Island. A legendary standing-room-only bacaro known for its iconic €1 mini-paninis and extremely cheap glasses of local wine (ombra).
    Osteria al Cicheto Authentic Venetian €€ 25 min transit / 15 min drive (8.5 km) 📍 Map A small, cozy hideaway near the Venice station. Serves outstanding, scratch-made local classics like baccalà mantecato (creamed salt cod) and sarde in saor.
