    Day 16 — Sunday, Sept 27 🛫

    Departure to Toronto

    Base: Transit (Madrid → Toronto)

    🎒 Overview

    The epic journey comes to a close. After hiking through the dramatic peaks of the Dolomites and raving in Madrid, it's time to head home.

    😴 Morning: Recovery

    • Check-Out: Depending on what time the Anyma concert ended, you might be moving a little slowly this morning!
    • Final Breakfast: Grab a strong coffee and a final Spanish breakfast (maybe some pan con tomate).
    • Pack: Make sure all bags are packed.

    ✈️ Afternoon: Airport Logistics

    • Transit to MAD: Take a taxi, Uber/Cabify, or the Metro (Line 8) to Adolfo Suárez Madrid–Barajas Airport (MAD). Budget about 30-45 minutes from the center of Madrid.
    • Check-In: Arrive at the airport 3 hours before your flight to Toronto (YYZ).
    • Hasta Luego: Board your flight back to Canada and start planning the next adventure!
